This project stemmed from my dislike of viewing pdf's on mobile devices. I decided to create a boilerplate that allows people to create simple responsive showcases of work. Not meant to replace a formal resume, but provide a simple online precense for professionals.

You can view a sample resume, and learn more on the project page
